About me

I’m a Devops Engineer and currently working at Expedia. I’m a big advocate of open source technology and love Linux, Docker, Python & automation. More recently I have become interested in Cloud in particular AWS and related technologies such as Terraform and Packer.

My passion for technology does not end at work !! For example, I built a baby monitor for my son using Raspberry Pi so I could see him on our smart TV or any of our smartphones !!
